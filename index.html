PET Computer Science Club

Pierre Elliott Trudeau High School's Computer Science Club

About Us


The PET Computer Science club aims to provide students with a platform to learn more about computer science and technology. We hold weekly meetings every Friday in room 220 and 223 for students of all skill levels. Whether you are a beginner or have been coding for a while, we welcome you to join us!
